Spot Destination

Balay Negrense

Found in Silay City, this museum is a well-preserved ancestral house that showcases the lifestyle of the Negrense elite during the 19th century. It provides a glimpse into the province's history and cultural heritage.

Mambukal Mountain Resort

Situated in Murcia, this mountain resort offers hot springs, waterfalls, and various recreational activities. It's a great place for nature lovers and those seeking relaxation.

Ruins

The Ruins is the remains of the ancestral home mansion of the family of Don Mariano Ledesma Lacson and Maria Braga Lacson. It is situated in Talisay, Negros Occidental, Philippines.
